About Our Getaway!

Thanks for stopping by!

My husband and I purchased RC’s Getaway to have a place to go to relax and unwind from the daily grind of work and living in the city. Not only do we love relaxing and spending time together on the beach, we also enjoy being able to share our getaway with others.

RC’s Getaway is located next to Russell-Fields Pier, and across the street from Pier Park which offers a multitude of shopping and eating options, as well as things to do, such as water rides, ferris wheel, indoor gaming, and movie theater. Whether you need a family friendly place to bring the kids or you want a fun place to enjoy some nightlife, Panama City Beach has something for everyone!

Check out our recommendations below or visit the city’s tour website to learn more.
